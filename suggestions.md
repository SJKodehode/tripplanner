# Feature Suggestions

## 1. Itinerary Battle
Show two candidate day plans side-by-side and let friends vote on their favorite.  
Use votes to automatically pick the winning itinerary.

## 3. Trip Bingo
Generate destination-specific challenges (for example: try a street food, sunrise photo, local market).  
Users check off completed items and earn points during the trip.

## 6. Rain Plan Button
Add a one-click fallback that swaps outdoor activities for indoor alternatives when weather turns bad.  
Keep timing, budget, and travel distance as close as possible to the original plan.

## 8. Memory Timeline
After the trip, convert itinerary events, check-ins, and photos into a shareable timeline recap.  
Include a simple export/share view for friends and social posting.
